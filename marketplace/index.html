Submit Your Addon

We're building a community addon registry. Submit your addon by opening a PR in the panel repository.

1

Add your addon entry to config.json

Fork the repository and add your addon details to the addons array in config.json.

2

Add screenshots

Drop images into public/assets/addons/your-addon-id/ and push to your fork.

3

Open a Pull Request

Open a PR against the main branch. Once reviewed and merged, it appears in the marketplace automatically.
